1 Gold Unit:

Footman (Strength: 1)
    When played: Reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a "Footman", draw it into your hand. Otherwise, discard it or place it on top of your deck.


2 Gold Unit:

Bowman (Strength: 2)
    When played: Discard 1 card from your hand to destroy 1 opposing unit. (There must be an opposing unit to trigger this ability.)


3 Gold Units:

Knight (Strength: 3)
    When played: Reveal the top card of your deck. If it is a unit with strength 3 or higher, discard it. Otherwise draw it into your hand.

Novice Wizard (Strength: 2)
    When played: Discard 1 card from your hand to draw 1 card.


5 Gold Unit:

Siege Machine (Strength: 4)
    Resolution: Discard 1 card from your hand to add 2 strength to this unit.


6 Gold Legendary Unit:

Roc Warrior (Strength: 5)
    When played: Return 1 friendly unit in this combat to your hand (other than a Roc Warrior).
