1 Gold Unit:

Fairy (Strength: 1)
   Resolution : Add 1 strength to this unit for each resolution ability that has been triggered by you during this combat. You may wound this unit.

2 Gold Unit:

Phoenix (Strength: 2)
   When played : Directly add a fairy from your barracks to your hand


3 Gold Units:

Cupid (Strength: 3)
    When played : If the opponent has 1 card or less in his hand, reveal as many cards as engaged fairies in the fight. Take all fairies in your hand and discard the others cards

Nephilim (Strength: 3)
   Resolution : Wound all fairies to destroy an ennemy unit.


5 Gold Unit:

Griffin (Strength: 4)
    When played : Your opponent plays 2 units, then you play 2 units. Each time, only the second unit can activate his 'when played' ability.


6 Gold Legendary Unit:

Archangel (Strength: 5)
    When played : Choose a number. Each player can discard up to that many card ans draw as many cards as discarded
