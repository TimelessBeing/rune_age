1 Gold Unit:

Deepwood Archer (Strength: 1)
    When played: Refresh 1 of your cities or strongholds.


2 Gold Unit:

Storm Sorceress (Strength: 2)
    Action: Claim this card as a reward. (Exhaust this card to generate 2 influence.) Discard this card at the end of the turn.

3 Gold Units:

Darnati Warrior (Strength: 3)
    When played: Spend 1 influence to destroy 1 opposing unit.

Leonx Rider (Strength: 3)
    When played: Spend 1 influence to choose any player. The player chooses and discards 1 card from his hand. If you choose yourself, you may draw 1 card before discarding.


5 Gold Unit:

Pegasus Rider (Strength: 4)
    When played: Spend 2 influence to reveal the top 2 cards of your deck. Draw 1 into your hand and discard the other.


6 Gold Legendary Unit:

Forest Guardian (Strength: 4)
    This unit cannot be wounded or destroyed in combat.
    The abilities of friendly units in this combat cost 1 less influence to use.
